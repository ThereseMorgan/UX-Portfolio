“Beauty is in the heart of the beholder”
H.G. Wells

I believe in experiential learning and have made it a point to explore a wide variety of interests, including being the bowman on a semi-professional sailing team, rock climbing, beekeeping, woodworking, and swing dancing to name a few! I have also traveled extensively, living in France, South Korea and Zambia.

I’m grateful to have been raised with cardboard boxes and open countryside, encouraged to be creative and inquisitive. I’m even more thankful that while my family experienced a lot of ups and downs, my parents remained steadfast examples of perseverance and growing through failures. For that I owe them everything.

As an artist, I focused on painting portraits of those close to me. With an initial major in psychology, I was naturally drawn to the experience of careful observation. The practice of painting further honed my level of empathy, and has forever deepened the way I view the world.
