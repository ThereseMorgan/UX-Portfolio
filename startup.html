Seqrpay - Austin Tech Startup

Role: UI Designer, Content Strategist

Duration: 2 weeks

Overview

I was tasked with designing and prototyping a hi-fi responsive brochure site for the local startup Seqrpay. We had to externalize their vision with content copy, information architecture and layout design. This site needed to speak to and inform 2 different customer segments. We were asked to create branding and a design spec, a deck outlining research and design decisions, wire-frames and a high fidelity responsive website. We had to differentiate their product from existing competition and design flows that led to product adoption./p>

About

Seqrpay aims to go against big names such as Square, Toast, Paypal and Google/Android Pay. They are a re-imagined POS and mPOS system designed with small business in mind.


Design Challenge

When I was brought on, the startup was building their pitch deck for round A funding. The challenge was to test and validate their assumptions and design a brochure site that speaks to 2 customer segments (B2B and B2C). If successful, this product would ....... How might we design a brochure site.

____PERSONA DETAILS

Synthesizing user research





I like, I wish, What if

Using "I like, I wish, What if" we begain to visualize the goals of our users based on the aggregated data points.


Aaron Black

Black Bark's BBQ Foodtruck

Age: 34
Occupation: Owner and Chef
Status: Single
Location: Austin, TX

“I’m stressed and excited. I can’t wait to say that I’ve made it past this early stage and comfortably can call myself a small business owner.”


Pain Points

There are so many expenses to juggle with a foodtruck, especially since I am still paying off the equiptment, my student loans, etc. I feel on edge knowing I don't have a financial cushion. Dealing with finances takes half my energy.


Goals

I have had my truck for less than a year, but I'm motivated to say I've really made it in this competitive. I've always wanted to be an entrepreneur and share my family's traditions, but I am ready for the stress to go down.

Jamie Hartforth

Yoga Instructor | Environmental Activist

Age: 29
Occupation: Graphic Designer
Status: Married
Location: Austin, TX

“It matters to me who I support and feeling like I am part of my community."


Pain Points

I get easily frustrated when things aren't easy. I forget things at home all of the time and it would be such a relief to know my phone can handle payments anywhere.


Goals

I want to live a minimalist lifestyle and support small business. It makes me feel like I am really a part of the local scene.

Meet Aaron & Jamie

We chose to focus our efforts on a small business owner who runs a food truck, because we felt solving his needs would address the core needs.
